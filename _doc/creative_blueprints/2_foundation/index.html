Foundation Validated

Local service businesses spend 5-8 hours weekly on social media management without a clear ROI, struggling to create location-specific content that engages their community and drives qualified leads.

The Problem

Local service businesses waste 5-8 hours weekly bouncing between social platforms, creating generic posts with no local relevance, missing inbound lead messages, and lacking actionable reputation management—all while competitors streamline their presence and win more jobs.

The Solution

Plumbers, contractors, landscapers, and boutique shops lack a unified platform to manage multi-channel social presence, generate locally-relevant content, capture inbound leads, and track reputation—forcing them to juggle disconnected tools or abandon social entirely.

Target Audience

Independent plumbers, contractors, landscapers, and boutique retail owners (1–10 employees) in mid-sized markets like Spartanburg, SC seeking efficient social management without hiring a dedicated marketer.

Personal Motivation

Empower local pros to compete with larger chains by automating content creation and lead management while maintaining authentic, location-driven messaging that builds community trust.
