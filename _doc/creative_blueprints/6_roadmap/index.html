Three high-impact capabilities staged for the next build sprint

Post-MVP

🗺️ Google Business Profile Sync

Owners see, respond to, and track Google reviews directly inside LocalPulse. AI SEO responses auto-draft for each review; one-tap posting pushes the reply back to Google — the channel where local reputation is won or lost.

SEO Impact
2
📸 Job-Site Before/After Photo Posts

Owner snaps two photos on-site, AI generates a hyper-local caption with trade keywords, and the post is injected into the publishing queue in one tap. Before/after photos are the highest-converting content type for local trades.

Top Content
3
👥 Team Collaboration Workspace

Business owner invites an office manager or apprentice, assigns roles (Admin / Poster / Viewer), and tracks who approved each post or replied to each lead via a built-in activity log. Removes the owner as the daily social media bottleneck.
